Blog - Securify Red Team

Security related writeups and advisories from Securify Red Team.

What is in this blog?

Securify's Red Team works with multiple companies to test their security service. In this blog post, you will find different writeups on vulnerabilities that we have identified in our testings. Some of these will be 0days that our team has discovered while some are fixed vulnerabilities that we found on organizations..

All of the writeups posted here have received consent from the organization to whom we reported this issue. If you are a member of organization who is mentioned in any of this blog and believe that we have made a mistake, please email redteam@securifyinc.com or fill the following contact page so that we can get it fixed.

About Securify Red Team

Securify Red Team, is a designated group in our company who work as pentesters. They break codes and work to find vulnerabilties from Medium to Critical severity. This team contains security researchers who are active in the industry and have expertise in web application security. Following are some blog posts that we have written for you.
