About Us

Securify Inc. is located in Fullerton, CA. We have expertise in security testing of web applications. Our idea is of hacking applications and finding vulnerabilities before the bad guys do.

Our Team

Our team consists of security engineers, security analysts and security researchers who have experiences in wide amount of applications from web apps to IoT services.

Our Work

Our security team has experience testing application securities as well as running managed bug bounty programs for companies. We have been recognized by multiple government organizations and private companies like: Uber, Snapchat, Google, Department of Defense, Yahoo, Slack, General Motors and many more. We have worked with more than 100 third party services to discover 0days that could have created serious impact to their customers.
